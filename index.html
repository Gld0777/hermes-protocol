Frequently Asked Questions

Which social apps are you compatible with?

We're starting of with Discord and Telegram. Afterwards we'll support Twitter and others. Feel free to tell us which one you'd love to see!

Which dApps can I subscribe to events?

We aim to support all Terra dApps by EoY, but become multi-chain native eventually.

How much will it cost me?

In theory? Nothing. We will ask users to make a small one-time deposit once they which to subscribe to certain events, and can withdraw their UST when they wish to no longer use our service.

Will you support SMS notifications?

Absolutely! Bear in mind these can become quite costly, however, so we're going to need to ask users to make a bigger deposit than what would otherwise be needed.

What about my Smart Home appliances? Lightbulbs, music player, etc.

By using popular integration services such as IFTTT, Zapier, Integromat and more, you'll have virtually infinite choices on how and where you want to be notified.
